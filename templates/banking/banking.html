Welcome to UnoBank, where we redefine what it means to bank with simplicity, speed, and security. At UnoBank, we believe that managing your finances should be stress-free, accessible, and empowering. With our user-first approach, advanced technology, and transparent services, UnoBank is here to make every transaction seamless and every goal within reach.

Deposit

A deposit is the act of adding funds to your account, increasing the available balance. This transaction can be made in cash, via bank transfer, or other payment methods.

Transfer

A transfer is the movement of funds from one account to another, either within the same bank or across different banks. This transaction is used to send money securely to others or between personal accounts.

Withdrawal

A withdrawal involves taking funds out of your account, reducing the available balance. This can be done through ATMs, bank branches, or electronic transfers.
